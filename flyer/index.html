Topics of Interest

The topics of interest for CIT-4-OSS include (but are not limited to) computational intelligence techniques applied in:

  • Behind-the-meter renewable power disaggregation
  • Distributed Energy Resources (DER) Visibility and Monitoring
  • Advanced monitoring for situational awareness, grid resilience and DER integration
  • Non-intrusive Load Monitoring
  • Interpretable, fair, and explainable AI for Smart Energy Systems
  • Distributed and collaborative intelligence for Smart Grid monitoring and control
  • Predictive and Proactive maintenance for Smart Grid infrastructure
  • AI-driven energy management solutions (e.g., inclusive demand flexibility, e-mobility justice, sustainable agriculture)
  • Smart charging for grid stability and load balancing
  • Generative AI for empowering power grid monitoring and stability
  • Forecasting of energy generation, demand, and load profiles
  • Collaborative Intelligence for energy monitoring
  • Power signal analysis for anomaly detection (e.g., disturbances, energy theft, power quality)
  • Computational Intelligence for integrating and stabilizing renewable energy sources (e.g., large-scale integration, power fluctuation mitigation)
